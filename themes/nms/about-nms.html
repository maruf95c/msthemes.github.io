About NMS Technologies

NMS Technologies Ltd. starts its Journey since 2017 for giving service and support to its customers. The array of this organization Services & supports are in the field of Information Technology, Communications and Securities with strength experience in handling the ever changing corporate scenario. Strong customer relationship, ability to provide the cutting edge technology and an excellent service & support infrastructure has been key focus of the organization to provide bestvalue-for-money. Today NMS is the idle of country's premier information enabling company in the mentioned field.

NMS offers to all valued customers like large multi-location enterprise, or a small/medium enterprise, or a small office or a home. It has Central Zone License to provide dedicated Internet & Data Connectivity, a product range, sales & support capability to serve the needs of the customer.

Leading market presence, continuous focus on market needs, design and development of industry standard solutions, prototyping ability and performance leadership have enabled NMS's experienced engineering teams to deliver state of the art solutions to allow businesses to move forward with their strategic objectives.
